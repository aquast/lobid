lobid is implemented and maintained by hbz's Linked Open Data group.

In addition to the lobid services, we work on search applications based on the lobid APIs and technologies in NWBib and digitalisiertedrucke, and on tools for data processing in Metafacture and the Metafacture IDE.

We participate in the organization of the annual SWIB conference and are active members in open source and standards organizations like the Eclipse Foundation and the World Wide Web Consortium.

Current members of the LOD group are Adrian Pohl, Pascal Christoph, Fabian Steeg, and Christoph Ewertowski.
